How to play

  • The goal is to guess the correct sequence of colors.
  • The history display will display your guesses, and the feedback of the guesses.
  • A black circle indicates that one of the colors is in the right position.
  • A white cirlce indicates that one of the colors is the right color, but in the wrong liosition.
  • The order of the white and black circles is meaningless.
  • See the Wikipedia page for more details.
  • Good luck!
